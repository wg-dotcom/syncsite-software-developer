Batch 1 · A note on selection

You need a developer who fits into a real delivery team, not just a resume that looks good.

The core of what Syncsite does is turning out-of-the-box CRM into something that actually works for a specific manufacturing or distribution business. That requires developers who solve problems in context, communicate clearly in writing, and can use AI tools to move faster — not as a crutch, but as a multiplier.

Every candidate here was screened for exactly that. We started with the technical foundation: C#, .NET, SQL Server, ASP.NET, and the ability to think through integration logic. From there we looked at communication, problem-solving under pressure, and whether they would operate effectively in a mostly async, email-first environment.

The search focused on Latin America to give you real CST overlap without the coordination friction. Each candidate can commit to your 8am–5pm schedule and has demonstrated the initiative and discipline to work in a remote-first setup. Your team stays stable. The work keeps moving.
